Sajek Valley - The Cloud Nine

Sajek Valley is a mesmerizing hill station located in Rangamati, Bangladesh. The valley is surrounded by lush green hills, clear blue skies, and a sea of clouds that create a surreal and scenic environment. It is often referred to as the "Queen of Hills" due to its breathtaking views and peaceful ambiance.

Top Features:

  • Mountain Trekking: Go on a guided trek through the hills and experience nature at its finest.
  • Cloud Viewing: Sajek Valley is renowned for its beautiful views of the clouds that engulf the hills.
  • Traditional Village Experience: Visit indigenous villages and learn about the local culture and lifestyle.

Transport Shubidhas:

  • By Bus: Buses are available from Dhaka to Rangamati, and from there, you can take a local jeep or car to Sajek Valley.
  • By Car: You can drive from Dhaka to Sajek Valley, but the roads are quite bumpy, so be prepared for an adventure!
  • By Air: Fly to Rangamati from Dhaka and take a car from the airport to reach Sajek Valley.
