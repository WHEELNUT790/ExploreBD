Sylhet - Land of Tea Gardens

Sylhet, located in northeastern Bangladesh, is a lush green region known for its rolling tea gardens, crystal-clear rivers, and scenic hills. It's a peaceful retreat for travelers looking to experience nature and tranquility.

Top Features:

  • Tea Gardens: Visit the picturesque tea estates that stretch across miles of lush landscape.
  • Ratargul Swamp Forest: One of the few swamp forests in Bangladesh, offering boat rides through submerged trees.
  • Jaflong: A scenic area near the Indian border known for its stone collection and clear rivers.

Transport Shubidhas:

  • By Bus: Sylhet is connected to major cities by deluxe and regular buses, taking 6-8 hours from Dhaka.
  • By Train: Intercity trains run regularly from Dhaka to Sylhet with different classes.
  • By Flight: Flights from Dhaka to Sylhet take only about 45 minutes and operate daily.
