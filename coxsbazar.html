Cox's Bazar - The Longest Beach

Cox's Bazar is famous for having the longest unbroken sea beach in the world, stretching over 120 kilometers. Located in the southeastern part of Bangladesh, it is one of the most popular tourist destinations for those seeking a beautiful coastal experience with golden sands and picturesque sunsets.

Top Features:

  • Beach Activities: Enjoy swimming, sunbathing, and beach volleyball along the vast beach.
  • Visit Himchori Waterfall: Explore the beautiful waterfalls near Cox's Bazar and enjoy the scenic views.
  • Fishing Trips: Take a boat ride into the Bay of Bengal for a traditional fishing experience.

Transport Shubidhas:

  • By Bus: Several buses are available from Dhaka to Cox's Bazar. The journey takes approximately 12-14 hours.
  • By Train: You can take a train from Dhaka to Chittagong, and from there take a bus to Cox's Bazar.
  • By Flight: A flight from Dhaka to Cox's Bazar is a convenient option, taking about 1 hour.
