Comilla - City of Heritage

Comilla is one of the oldest cities in Bangladesh, rich in historical significance and culture. It features ancient archaeological sites like Mainamati, beautiful parks, and traditional rural life.

Top Features:

  • Mainamati Ruins: Explore the ancient Buddhist archaeological site with stupas and temples.
  • Comilla Cantonment: Visit one of the most organized cantonments in the country.
  • Rupban Mura: A peaceful and scenic location with historical relics and natural charm.

Transport Shubidhas:

  • By Bus: Comilla is well-connected with frequent bus services from Dhaka and Chittagong.
  • By Train: Several intercity trains run daily from Dhaka to Comilla.
  • By Private Car: You can take the Dhaka-Chittagong highway for a smooth road journey.
