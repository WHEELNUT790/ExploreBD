Sundarbans - The Majestic Mangroves

The Sundarbans is the world's largest mangrove forest, located in the southwestern part of Bangladesh. It is home to a variety of unique wildlife, including the majestic Royal Bengal Tiger. The forest is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and offers a remarkable journey for nature lovers and adventure seekers.

Top Features:

  • Royal Bengal Tiger Safari: Get a chance to spot the famous Bengal Tiger in its natural habitat.
  • Boat Tours: Take a boat tour through the river channels and explore the dense forest.
  • Bird Watching: Sundarbans is home to over 200 bird species, making it a birdwatcher's paradise.

Transport Shubidhas:

  • By Bus: You can take a bus from Dhaka to Khulna, the nearest city to the Sundarbans.
  • By Train: Train services are available from Dhaka to Khulna, with daily departures.
  • By Boat: There are boat services that take tourists from Khulna to various parts of the Sundarbans.
  • By Flight: You can take a flight to Jessore or Khulna, then proceed by road to the Sundarbans.
